Teaching Presentations

Directions

Teaching presentations are organized by week. Claim a topic by going to the sign-up page (in this module) and signing up for a topic. Selection is done on a first-come first-serve basis by editing the document and adding your own name to the right of the topic in the column for your section. Do NOT replace someone else's name with your own!

Be sure to record your topic and date into a calendar with a reminder in order to be prepared for your presentation when it is due.

Requirements

Develop a 7-minute (maximum) teaching presentation where you will teach others about the topic. You may use any medium for your presentation as long as it facilitates meeting the requirements listed below:

The presentation must meet the following requirements:

Campus Requirements

  1. The presentation will be at the beginning of class on the scheduled day.
  2. The presentation must demonstrate how the topic is used in web development.
  3. The presentation must contain a working example of the topic.

Online Requirements

  1. The presentation will be a video recording (verbal explanation must be part of the video). Refer to the Media Resource document as needed.
  2. The video must be posted online to YouTube.
  3. The video must demonstrate how the topic is used in web development.
  4. The video must contain a working example of the topic.
  5. A link to the teaching presentation must be posted by Tuesday evening, 11:59pm mountain time, to that week's discussion board.
