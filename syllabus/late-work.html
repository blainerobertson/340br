Late Work Policy

Work is due as specified by the course calendar and indicated in the weekly overview pages and assessment descriptions. Typically assessments are due Saturday evenings at 11:59pm Mountain Time (America/Boise time zone).

What is late?

Work is considered late when not submitted:

  1. on time, or
  2. in an incomplete form, or
  3. submitting the wrong assessment.

Test

Be sure to check links to videos for operability and that the correct assessment was submitted to the correct location. It is your responsibility to make sure things are correct.

Penalty

Work that is late is penalized 50%. Late work may be submitted through Saturday of week 12.

Notification

When submitting late work you must also email the professor indicating that a late submission has been made and be sure to specify the name of the late assessment.
